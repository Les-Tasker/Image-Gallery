Route 360

Route 360, an off-road jungle trek to the highest point in Koh Samui with a viewpoint overlooking the east side of the island.

Ang Thong

A 5 minute boat trip from Koh Tao is the Ang Thong Marine Reserve. A beautiful island surrounded by crystal clear water and a huge variety of marine life. The perfect place for a spot of snorkeling.

Sunset by Angelina

Just a short walk from the "Big Buddah" temple in the northern part of Koh Samui you will find this small bar and restaurant. A perfect spot in the evening for a romantic meal overlooking the Samui sunset.

Fishermans Village Night Market

Wednesday nights come alive in the Fishermans Village. With a gigantic market full of anything and everything, you could spend a good amount of time wandering the various stalls and watching the street performances situated in the center of the market.

#WeRentedaScooter

A trip to Thailand is all but complete without renting a scooter. From the relief of the heat as you're zipping about, to the freedom to stop anywhere and make your own discoveries.
